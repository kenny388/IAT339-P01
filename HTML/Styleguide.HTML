My User-flow Task : To Find a product in the product listing page, check its detail and purchase it

Please follow the steps below to walk through my user flow in my wireframe:

  1. In the Home page, please click any of the categories on the side, or products on the main navigation bar
  2. In the product listing page, please click on one of the products
  3. In the product detail page, please click add to cart. For now, the wireframe would direct users straight to the shopping cart
  4. In the shopping cart, please proceed to advance to the payment information forms
  5. In the payment information page, please type in essential information and then click proceed
  6. The purchase is done, your order is placed
